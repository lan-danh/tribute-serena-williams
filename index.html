ATHLETE

Serena Jameka Williams is an American professional tennis player and former world No. 1 in women's single tennis. She has won 23 Grand Slam singles titles, the most by any player in the Open Era, and the second-most of all time behind Margaret Court.

FASHIONISTA

Serena launched her first proprietary fashion collection in 2018, simply called “Serena,” Anchoring the line are messages of self-love and confidence. The line, which is being sold exclusively on her own web site, is centered around practicality.

MOTHER

Serena and Alexis Ohanian have a three year old daughter named Olympia. Serena frequently posts their family adventures on Instagram, which may win them the cutest family award. Olympia has been seen cheering for her mom at tennis tournaments.
